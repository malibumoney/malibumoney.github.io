What is CAIRN?
CAIRN is your personal companion in the Nationl Parks. CAIRN shows hikers all relevant information about trails so they are prepared and ready to take on an adventure! We designed CAIRN to be easy to use and free of advertisments. We do not sell your personal data. CAIRN is adventurous and honest, just like you.
