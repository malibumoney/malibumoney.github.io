Malibu Money Club

Malibu Money Club is nestled high in the Malibu hills to provide maximum privacy and stunning panoramic views of the Malibu riviera. The Club is a modern and inspirational space with fine dining, a relaxing terrace, and a state of the art music studio. Malibu Money Club was designed to be a fresh take on the American country club. The Club is open year round to members. Membership is by invitation only.
